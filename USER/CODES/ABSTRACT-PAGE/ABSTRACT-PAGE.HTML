Abstract

This project study, entitled "Sharma Burger ala Lasagna," is a food product that combines well-known delicacies from all around the world into a single product. We decided to merge those three foods in order to create a unique product. We utilized mashed potato for the bun, instead of the traditional bun that you would expect to see in the burger structure, and we accompanied it with a sauce. This product may be recommended for the masses because of its affordable price, which is within the reach of most people's budgets and also worth the expense of purchasing. It also has a lot of benefits because the food is fresh and tasty. The purpose of this research is to determine if the Shawarma Burger ala Lasagna is feasible in the market. The main focus area will be the Liceo de Cagayan University-Main Campus, which is located at Kauswagan High-Way and Rodolfo N. Pelaez Blvd. According to the researchers, the population of Liceo de Cagayan University Main Campus, according to the researchers, will be a tremendous market for Shawarma Burger ala Lasagna, which is an opportunity that should not be missed. Furthermore, they would be suitable customers in assessing whether or not selling our product is indeed a feasible option in the near future. The study will be carried out via a comprehensive online survey, which allows us to make the assumption that selling Shawarma Burger ala Lasagna is feasible and profitable. With a total of 100 participants, they will be selected from grades 11 and 12 ABM, STEM, and COA to ensure that each grade level and strand is adequately represented. Following the collection of data and findings, the researchers' analysis and computation demonstrate that the sales of Shawarma Burger ala Lasagna are feasible and profitable in the market.
